🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE: Generate a Pixar-Style Explainer Video Script
====================================================================

🎯 GOAL:
Create a Pixar-style animated explainer video script using an onboarding document and a 3D mascot design.
The final script will be divided into short, visual scenes ready for AI video generation tools like Kling AI and Hailuo AI.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✅ WHAT YOU NEED:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. 📄 A structured onboarding document
2. 🦈 A high-quality image of the mascot

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🔁 STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📂 STEP 1: Upload the Onboarding Document
------------------------------------------
Prompt:
> “This is our onboarding document. Please read and understand the content.”

🖼️ STEP 2: Upload the Mascot Image
-----------------------------------
Prompt:
> “Here is the design of the mascot that will be the main character of the video. 
> Please analyze the image and write a short, vivid description of the mascot suitable for use in prompts.
> Include the mascot’s appearance, expression, outfit, and any unique traits.”

ChatGPT will return a reusable mascot prompt such as:
> A sleek light grey and white anthropomorphic shark with big expressive eyes, a wide toothy smile, 
> wearing a beige shirt, denim cargo shorts, and brown work boots.

✂️ STEP 3: Break the Document into Scenes
------------------------------------------
Prompt:
> “Split the onboarding content into short video scenes. Each scene should explain only ONE idea.
> The voiceover for each scene must be one sentence that fits into 5 seconds of narration.”

🧠 STEP 4: Generate the Script
-------------------------------
Prompt:
> “For each scene, provide:
> 1. Scene number
> 2. The 5-second voiceover sentence
> 3. A detailed graphics prompt, including the **exact mascot description you generated earlier in Step 2**.  
> Make sure the mascot is shown performing an action appropriate to the scene's voiceover.  
> The background should be imaginative or educational to fit the tone.”

Each scene should look like:

Scene 3:

Voiceover:  
"AI helps with talking, deciding, and even solving problems!"

Graphics prompt:  
An underwater meeting space with floating holographic icons and bright coral walls. 
A sleek light grey and white anthropomorphic shark with big expressive eyes, a wide toothy smile, wearing a beige shirt and denim cargo shorts,
 points to the icons with excitement. Pixar 3D style, high detail, clean lighting.

🎬 STEP 5: Add Video Generation Prompts
----------------------------------------
Prompt:
> “Now, for each scene, add short prompts for Kling AI and Hailuo AI.
> These prompts should be one sentence and only describe the mascot’s physical action (not the setting or background).”

Example:

Video generation prompts:  
Kling AI:  
The shark points at icons and smiles.  
Hailuo AI:  
The shark points at icons and smiles.

📦 STEP 6: Export the Script
-----------------------------
Prompt:
> “Now export the full script as a downloadable .txt file.”

🧾 FINAL FORMAT PER SCENE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Scene 4:

Voiceover:  
"From Turing’s ideas to smart assistants — AI has come a long way!"

Graphics prompt:  
A futuristic underwater gallery with smooth coral pillars and large floating holographic screens. 
The first screen shows a portrait of Alan Turing, the second displays a glowing microphone representing voice assistants, 
and the third screen features a laptop with a smart cloud symbol. Floating light trails connect all three screens. 
The background includes vibrant sea plants and colorful fish. 
A sleek light grey and white anthropomorphic shark with big expressive eyes and a wide toothy smile, wearing a beige button-up short-sleeve shirt, 
denim cargo shorts, and brown work boots, stands proudly gesturing toward the timeline with enthusiasm. 
Bright and clean lighting, Pixar-like 3D cartoon style, high detail, elegant and educational.

Video generation prompts:  
Kling AI:  
The shark gestures proudly toward the floating timeline.  
Hailuo AI:  
The shark gestures proudly toward the floating timeline.

💡 That’s it! Use this guide to generate video scripts anytime you have a new onboarding document and mascot.
